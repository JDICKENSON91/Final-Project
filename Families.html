Families
89.8% of the population of the Town of Victoria Park was part of an intact family in 2016. A further 9% were either blended or step families.
As the population ages the population transitions from a huge proportion of people never being married to either still being married or divorced. This ends with the last group prodomintley being widowed after the age of 75.
The divorced population has largely remained the same between 2006 and 2016 but people who are married or have never married have grown in the past three Census periods. The divorced population remaining steady could be due to more people choosing not to marry.
