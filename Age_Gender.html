Most of the population of the Town of Victoria Park belongs to Generation Y / Gen Next, born between 1980 and 1994. Gen Y make up 31.7% of the population, iGen/Gen Z is the next biggest group with 17.1% just slightly more than Baby Boomers sitting at 16%.
For the last 20 years there has been a massive spike of population for people between 18 and 30, showing that young families and students are moving into the the Town of Victoria Park.
There were slightly more females than males living in the Town of Victoria Park during the last Census but the split between genders has consistently been very close for the last three Census periods.
