A survey was conducted asking residents of the town to rate The Town of Victoria on a range of factors including;
Shopping, Culture and Cafes and Restaurants were the three most prominent factors that contributed to a person being a happy resident in the Town of Victoria Park. Whilst low scores in Schools and Access to Employment contributed to people not feeling happy.
The Town of Victoria Park's Culture and Shopping categories received the highest average scores in comparison to the other categories..
The importance of the Restaruants, Cafes and Culture referenced with the need for Schools and Access to Employment is most likely caused by the growing population of Young Families and Students.
